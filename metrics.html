How Impact Is Calculated

  • ROI = (Total Financial Benefit - Total Investment) / Total Investment x 100
  • Conversion Lift = (Post-launch Conversion - Baseline Conversion) / Baseline Conversion x 100
  • Operational Efficiency Gain = (Baseline Time or Cost - New Time or Cost) / Baseline Time or Cost x 100
  • Default Reduction = (Baseline Default Rate - New Default Rate) / Baseline Default Rate x 100
  • SLA Compliance = Completed Within SLA / Total Eligible Transactions x 100
  • Uptime = (Total Service Time - Downtime) / Total Service Time x 100
  • Risk Reduction = (Baseline Risk Exposure - New Risk Exposure) / Baseline Risk Exposure x 100
