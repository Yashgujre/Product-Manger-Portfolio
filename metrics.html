How Impact Is Calculated

ROI

ROI = (Total Benefit - Total Investment) / Total Investment x 100

Conversion Lift

Conversion Lift = (Post-Launch Conversion - Baseline Conversion) / Baseline Conversion x 100

Operational Efficiency

Operational Efficiency = (Baseline Time or Cost - New Time or Cost) / Baseline Time or Cost x 100

Uptime

Uptime = (Total Service Time - Downtime) / Total Service Time x 100
