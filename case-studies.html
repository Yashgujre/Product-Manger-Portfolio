Case Studies

Real project outcomes

Each case follows Problem, Process, Architecture, Metrics, and Tech Stack from your source content.

Centene

Problem: Fragmented referral workflows caused major delays for Medicaid interventions.

Process: Discovery across 12 nonprofits in 4 states with interviews, ride-alongs, and financial analysis.

Outcome: 74% faster referral acknowledgement and 60% fewer ER visits for housed members.

Capital One

Problem: 2-7 day manual B2B credit review cycles hurt conversion and risk consistency.

Process: Build/Buy/Partner strategy and API-first phased implementation.

Outcome: 8-12% conversion lift, 22% default reduction, and $529M+ year-one benefit.

Galderma

Problem: 80% of critical supply-chain data sat in downstream systems outside direct control.

Process: Chose orchestration over replacement and executed a 4-phase roadmap.

Outcome: 292% ROI, 70% manual reporting reduction, and +25 NPS points.

Prism Health

Problem: 47 grants, 15+ funders, and high compliance risk under spreadsheet operations.

Process: 42 interviews, process observations, compliance matrix, and HIPAA-compliant releases.

Outcome: Renewal rates improved to 91% and audit risk reduced by 78%.

UT Dallas

Problem: Paper-driven campus workflows created long queues and manual backlog.

Process: Built modern SPA facade over legacy systems with phased releases.

Outcome: 80% fewer office visits and 55% adoption increase.
