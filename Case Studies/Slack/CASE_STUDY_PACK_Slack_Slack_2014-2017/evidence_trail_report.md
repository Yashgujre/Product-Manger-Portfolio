# Evidence Trail Report
## Slack Technologies — Enterprise Communication Platform
## Case Window: February 2014 – December 2017
## Generated: 2025-01-01
## Pack Confidence: **Low**

**Purpose:** This report traces every piece of information used in the Research Pack back to its original source, states what the source explicitly said, and explains precisely how that evidence was used to satisfy a specific readiness pillar or sub-requirement.

**Organization:** Six readiness pillars, each containing a numbered evidence table. Every entry carries a provenance quality label and a claim confidence score. A Confidence Summary Table appears after all pillars.

**Provenance key:**
- 🟢 Verbatim, contemporaneous — direct quote from a source published during or near the case window (within ~2 years after close)
- 🟡 Paraphrased, contemporaneous — user's summary of a source published during or near the case window
- 🟠 Secondary aggregation — paraphrased from a synthesis/analysis of multiple primary sources
- 🔴 Retrospective — source published more than 2 years after the case window ends, describing past events

**Claim confidence key:**
- **High** — 🟢 or 🟡 contemporaneous source; no material provenance warnings
- **Medium** — paraphrased via secondary analysis, or retrospective source, or documented provenance warning (unverified date, paywall-limited, secondary pull)
- **Low** — solely retrospective AND carries an additional documented warning (single source, unverified, or contradicts window dates)
