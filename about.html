About Me

From Mumbai to Seattle, building products that matter.

Before product management, I started as an engineer. That path taught me where value lives: in closing the gap between what teams can build and what users truly need.

Narrative

I earned my Bachelor's in Electrical Engineering from the University of Mumbai, then moved to Dallas for my M.S. in Information Technology and Management at UT Dallas. My first major product ownership was UT Dallas' self-service portal for 30,000+ users, where we eliminated manual backlog and increased adoption by 55%.

I then shipped high-stakes products in regulated environments: real-time credit decisioning at Capital One, supply chain visibility with 292% ROI at Galderma, and grant compliance transformation at Prism Health. Now at Centene, I am scaling a B2B care coordination platform across 50+ nonprofits serving Medicaid populations.

Current Focus

I combine product strategy, execution discipline, and AI-augmented workflows to deliver measurable outcomes. I am also pursuing an MBA at Indiana Wesleyan University (expected December 2027) to deepen strategic and financial leadership.
