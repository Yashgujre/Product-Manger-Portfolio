## Objective
Define a scalable channel taxonomy and onboarding IA that improves discoverability and reduces setup confusion.

## IA Principles
- One purpose per channel.
- Naming conventions are required, not optional.
- Default channel sets vary by team archetype.

## Channel Taxonomy
- `#team-<function>` for functional updates
- `#proj-<initiative>` for delivery work
- `#ops-<domain>` for operational workflows
- `#ann-<org|region>` for announcements
- `#help-<topic>` for support and Q&A

## Onboarding Flow IA
1. Choose team archetype.
2. Auto-provision starter channel pack.
3. Suggest integration setup by archetype.
4. Display first-value checklist.

## Anti-Patterns
- Generic channels with mixed intent.
- No owner assigned to high-traffic channels.
- Deeply nested naming that harms retrieval.

## Acceptance Criteria
- 90%+ of new channels conform to naming rules.
- New teams complete channel setup in first session.
- Search success increases in first 30 days.
