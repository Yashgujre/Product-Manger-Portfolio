## Rollout Objective
Operationalize transition from successful team-level adoption to controlled enterprise-wide deployment.

## Rollout Governance
- Executive Sponsor: CIO/CTO equivalent
- Program Lead: Enterprise Product Manager
- Control Functions: Security, IT Admin, Compliance, Legal

## Phase Plan
### Phase 1: Grassroots Adoption (Months 1-6)
- Seed high-collaboration teams.
- Track invite rates, channel creation, and integration depth.
- Identify internal champions.

### Phase 2: Team Expansion (Months 6-12)
- Expand to adjacent functional teams.
- Standardize naming conventions, retention defaults, and admin roles.
- Capture cross-team collaboration wins.

### Phase 3: IT and Compliance Validation (Months 12-18)
- Validate SSO/SAML federation.
- Complete audit logging and DLP pathway checks.
- Finalize data residency and key management requirements.
- Dry-run migration playbooks from legacy systems.

### Phase 4: Enterprise Contract and Scale (Month 18+)
- Execute enterprise agreement.
- Roll out centralized admin controls.
- Launch organization-wide enablement and support model.

## Readiness Gates
- Security checklist complete.
- Legal/compliance approvals complete.
- Admin operations model staffed.
- KPI dashboard and escalation routing active.

## Hypercare Model (First 30 Days)
- Daily health review (adoption, incidents, support backlog).
- Dedicated triage queue for admin blockers.
- Weekly executive summary of rollout KPIs.

## Exit Criteria
- Adoption thresholds met across priority business units.
- Incident volumes normalized.
- Governance and compliance controls in steady-state operation.
