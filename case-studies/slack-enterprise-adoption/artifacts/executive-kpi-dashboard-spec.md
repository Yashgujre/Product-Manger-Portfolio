## Dashboard Objective
Provide leadership with a concise view of PLG health, enterprise rollout readiness, and business impact.

## Views
1. Growth Funnel: signup -> activation -> paid conversion.
2. Engagement Quality: DAU/MAU, active channels, integration usage.
3. Revenue and Expansion: conversion, NRR, expansion ARR.
4. Risk Signals: mute-rate, support ticket rate, integration failures.

## KPI Definitions
- Activation: workspace crosses 2,000 messages within 14 days.
- Engagement: DAU/MAU ratio.
- Revenue: free-to-paid conversion rate.
- Expansion: net revenue retention.

## Cadence and Distribution
- Weekly operating review deck.
- Monthly executive summary with trend deltas.
- Quarterly board-ready snapshot.

## Alerting Thresholds
- Activation drop >5 percentage points week-over-week.
- DAU/MAU drop >3 percentage points.
- Conversion decline >0.5 percentage points.
- Elevated support tickets per 1,000 users.
