## Objective
Run disciplined experimentation to increase activation, engagement, and conversion while protecting retention and trust metrics.

## Scope
- Onboarding funnel
- Notification defaults
- Search relevance
- Authentication friction
- Integration setup flow

## Guardrails
- No experiment may degrade 30-day retention by more than 1 percentage point.
- No experiment may increase mute-rate by more than 2 percentage points.
- Enterprise compliance controls remain unchanged during growth tests.

## Standard Experiment Template
- Hypothesis
- Segment
- Primary metric and minimum detectable effect
- Secondary metrics and guardrails
- Runtime and success criteria
- Rollout/rollback plan

## Governance
- Weekly experiment review with Product, Data Science, and Engineering.
- Decision outcomes: Rollout, Iterate, Hold, or Revert.
