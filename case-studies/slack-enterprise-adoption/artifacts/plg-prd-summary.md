## Product Objective
Convert self-serve team adoption into enterprise-grade expansion while maintaining high engagement and measurable business impact.

## North Star
Messages sent per organization per week.

## Strategic Goals
1. Reduce time-to-value to under 5 minutes for first meaningful collaboration event.
2. Increase activated teams crossing 2,000 messages within 14 days.
3. Improve free-to-paid conversion via value-aligned upgrade triggers.
4. Sustain expansion quality through 120%+ NRR.

## Target Users and Segments
- Team-level self-serve adopters.
- Cross-functional managers who coordinate multi-team projects.
- Enterprise admins and procurement stakeholders for organization-wide rollout.

## Core User Flows
1. Workspace creation and teammate invitation.
2. Channel setup by project, team, and function.
3. Integration onboarding for key tools.
4. Cross-team expansion and enterprise governance enablement.

## Functional Requirements
- Fast workspace setup and first message flow.
- Persistent channels with rich search and retention controls.
- Integration ecosystem with notifications and workflow handoffs.
- Role-based admin controls and enterprise policy support.

## Non-Functional Requirements
- Security and compliance controls for regulated enterprises.
- Reliable real-time delivery at scale.
- Search performance for large historical datasets.

## KPI Framework
- Activation: Teams reaching 2,000 messages in 14 days.
- Engagement: DAU/MAU.
- Revenue: Free-to-paid conversion.
- Expansion: NRR and account growth.
- Business: ROI and productivity outcomes.

## Experimentation Plan
- Onboarding sequence tests.
- Notification calibration tests.
- Search relevance tuning tests.
- Authentication friction reduction tests.

## Risks and Mitigations
- Risk: Activation stalls after setup.
  - Mitigation: Guided onboarding prompts and templates.
- Risk: Notification fatigue.
  - Mitigation: Adaptive defaults and user-level controls.
- Risk: Enterprise rollout blocked by compliance gaps.
  - Mitigation: Early security and governance tracks.

## Launch Readiness Gates
- Analytics and event taxonomy implemented.
- Security controls validated.
- Baseline KPI dashboard operational.
- Support and sales enablement complete.
